Marketing Manager

New York, USA Full Time $123 - $456

Job description

A manager is responsible for overseeing employees and implementing business strategies assigned by executives. They work in a leadership role for an organization and serve as a guide to lower-level team members, often motivating, coaching, and improving their employees' work performance. The following is a sample job description for a manager post:

Responsibility

The responsibilities of a manager vary depending on the industry and company, but some common responsibilities include:

  • Accomplishes department objectives by managing staff.
  • planning and evaluating department activities.
  • Project management.
  • Developing budgets.
  • Foster teamwork.
  • Handles pressure.
  • Giving feedback.

Qualifications

The qualifications for a manager position vary depending on the industry and company, but some common qualifications include:

  • Bachelor's degree in business, operations, finance, or a related field.
  • Strong leadership skills and the ability to manage a team effectively.
  • Excellent communication skills, both verbal and written.
  • Minimum of 4-8 years of work experience in program management field.
  • Willingness to travel between several offices within their territory to perform their duties.
